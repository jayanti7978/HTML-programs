NALANDA INSTITUE OF TECHNOLOGY

Nalanda Institute of Technology (NIT) was inaugurated in the year 2006 and is located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. NIT is affiliated with Bijuz Patnaik University of Technology, Rourkela.NIT offers B.Tech program of four-year duration with 6 specializations and M.Tech course of two-year duration with 4 specializations and intake of 18 students in each specialization.Students who have passed 10+2 with minimum 45% marks in aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, or Mathematics are eligible to get admission to B.Tech course. Those who have BE/ B.Tech Degree with minimum 60% marks and have qualified GATE are considered eligible to apply for admission to the M.Tech program.Admission to B.Tech course is given through JEE Main conducted by CBSE. Admission to the M.Tech program is done with valid GATE score and those students who do not have valid GATE score have to appear in the online entrance test conducted by NIT.Candidates are required to apply online for JEE Main for admission to B.Tech course respectively. The application form is made available on the official website of JEE Main and GATE Payment of the application fee shall be done through online or offline mode.
